Playstyles

Early playing used fingertips to pluck strings, which developed into fingerstyle and flamenco techniques. Flamenco uses aggressive strumming, percussive taps on the body, and rapid rhythmic patterns. Today, many players use plectrums (picks) for strumming and flatpicking.
